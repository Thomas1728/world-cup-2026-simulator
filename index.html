Teams reflect the Final Draw held 5 Dec 2025 (Washington, D.C.). Match codes (M73–M104), dates and bracket layout follow the official FIFA schedule. Third-place slots are filled by a constraint solver over FIFA's official eligibility table (the 3ABCDF-style labels) — it always returns a legal, rematch-free allocation for any 8 of the 12 thirds. Within the legal options the exact pairing can differ from FIFA's Annex C lookup in some scenarios. Your picks are saved automatically in this browser. Team names are easy to edit near the top of the GROUPS object in this file.
